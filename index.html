Task

"Prepare a 5 minute individual presentation on the main lessons you learnt from the Teenagers' group activities this year

This year was really amazing for the Chapel of Grace Teenagers. We had a lot of events, all of which were extremely wonderful

*****

My favorite out of all the activities was when we, the Teenagers, were given The Purpose Driven Life, a book written by Rick Warren, and were instructed to try and complete reading it in 40 days. (Which is how the book is meant to be read)

The Purpose Driven Life really changed how I viewed the world and taught me a lot of things which I will cherish in my heart forever. Such as:

  1. Having the mindset that Earth is not where we belong and we should not be distracted by the things of this world, constantly setting our eyes on the Heavenly Prize

  2. Recognizing GOD as your lifeline (Without GOD life makes no sense)

  3. GOD is our creator and we have been in his care even before we were born (Isaiah 44:2), so trust in Him

  4. We were placed on earth to prepare us for eternity

  5. The only accurate way to understand ourselves is by what GOD is and by what he does for us.

  6. We must be able to recognize use well our GOD given gifts

  7. Anything you do that brings pleasure to GOD is an act of worship

  8. Endeavour to make GOD smile by praising and thanking Him for all He has done

  9. Surrender your whole being to HIM to be used for righteous purposes

  10. Being Aware of others and putting them first

All this and more I have learnt from The Purpose Driven life.
